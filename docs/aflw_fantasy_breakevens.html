AFLW Fantasy Breakevens

Breakevens (BE) are an essential tool for AFLW Fantasy coaches, providing expected price changes for players. A break-even represents the score a player needs to score to retain their current price. The AFLW Fantasy pricing system moves faster to account for the 10-round season; below are estimates using 2023 data for expected price changes scoring 25, 50, 75, 100, 125 points.

Double game weeks: Double-game players have new BEs based on their score in Game 1 and the score they need in Game 2 to maintain their previous price.
