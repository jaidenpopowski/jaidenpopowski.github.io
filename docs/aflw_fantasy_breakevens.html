AFLW Fantasy Breakevens

Breakevens (BE) are an essential tool for AFLW Fantasy coaches, providing expected price changes for players. A break-even represents the score a player needs to score to retain their current price.

Double game weeks: Double-game players have BEs based on scoring identically in both games, or where their first games are over, recalculated to their 2nd game BE to keep their pre-round price.
